Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cryptobelievers?

Cryptobelievers is our platform native token used for various transactions and engagements within our Web3 Christian Community. It can be used in the marketplace, for donations, and other platform features.

How do I get started with the Bible feature?

Our Bible feature allows you to explore the words of the Bible like never before. Simply sign up or log in to your account, and navigate to the Bible section to begin your immersive experience.

What can I do on the Social Platform?

On our Social Platform, you can engage with Christians from around the globe by sharing pictures, memes, and videos. You can also network with like-minded individuals through comment sections and chats.

What is the Blessed Bazaar?

The Blessed Bazaar is our marketplace where you can buy and sell digital Christian materials such as self-help books, courses, audiobooks, digital art, and services using cryptobeliever Coin.

What kind of entertainment is available?

Our Christian Home Entertainment section offers curated gospel songs that praise and worship our creator. You can also view Christian videos with your family that teach Christian values and inspire you to better your own life.

How does Graceful Giving work?

Graceful Giving allows you to donate cryptobeliever Coins to charitable causes around the world. You will receive updates on the charity events you contributed to, ensuring transparency and engagement.

What are Sphere Bids?

Sphere Bids is a feature where you can earn by engaging in our rotary and bidding systems. Stand to win loads of token prizes whenever and wherever you are.

What is the Bible Space environment?

The Bible Space environment is an engaging 3D environment where you can explore with your customizable avatar. Learn about the Bible in a museum setting for each book, and discover various Bible stories and the Christian values they teach.
