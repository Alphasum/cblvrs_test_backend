Features

Bible
Explore the words of the Bible like never before with our enhanced User interface that immerses you and interacts with you.

Social Circle
Engage with christians from around the globe by sharing pictures, meme’s and video’s as you network with like minded individuals in comment sections and chats.

Blessed Bazaar
Buy and sell digital christian materials like self help books, courses, audio books, digital art and services using our platform native coin.

Christian Home Entertainment
Browse through our curated gospel songs that praise and worship our creator. View christian videos with your family that teach christian values to children and inspire you to better your own life.

Graceful Giving
Donate some tokens to charitable causes around the world using our platform native token and receive updates on the charity event you contributed to.

Sphere Bids and Lottery
Earn by engaging in our Lottery and bidding systems where you stand to win loads of token prizes whenever and wherever you are.

Bible Space environment
Explore our engaging 3D environment with your customizable avatar and learn about the bible in a museum setting for each and every book of the Bible. Learn about various Bible stories you never knew about and the christian values obtained from these stories.
