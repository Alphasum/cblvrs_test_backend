Community Guidelines

Welcome to our Web3 Christian Community website. Our mission is to create a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment where users can engage with the Bible, participate in social interactions, enjoy entertainment, and utilize our marketplace and rotary features. These guidelines are designed to ensure a positive experience for all members. Please read them carefully and adhere to them at all times.

Respectful Communication

We expect all users to communicate with respect and kindness. Any form of harassment, bullying, hate speech, or offensive behavior is strictly prohibited. Treat others as you would like to be treated.

Content Sharing

When sharing content, ensure that it aligns with our Christian values. Avoid posting anything that is inappropriate, harmful, or violates the rights of others. This includes, but is not limited to, images, videos, and written content.

Privacy and Personal Information

Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent. This includes addresses, phone numbers, and any other personal data.

Use of Christ Coin

Christ Coin is our platform native token. Use it responsibly and in accordance with our policies. Any fraudulent or unethical behavior related to Christ Coin will not be tolerated.

Marketplace Conduct

Our marketplace is a place to buy and sell digital Christian materials. Ensure that all transactions are conducted with integrity and honesty. Any form of scam or deceitful activity will result in immediate action.

Entertainment and Social Platform

Our entertainment section and social platform are designed for wholesome engagement. Avoid sharing content that is explicit, inappropriate, or not aligned with our community values. Engage in discussions that are uplifting and encouraging.

Reporting Violations

If you encounter any behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to our support team immediately. We take all reports seriously and will take appropriate action.

Consequences of Violations

Violating these guidelines can result in temporary or permanent suspension from the platform. We reserve the right to remove any content or user that does not adhere to these guidelines.
